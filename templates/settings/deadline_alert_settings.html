How Deadline Alerts Work

Deadline alerts help you stay on top of your project timelines. When enabled:

  • Alerts appear on your dashboard for active projects with end dates
  • Colors indicate urgency: blue for upcoming, orange for soon, red for urgent
  • Click on an alert to go directly to the project
  • Completed projects won't trigger alerts
