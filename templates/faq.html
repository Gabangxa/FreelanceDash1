Frequently Asked Questions

Freelancer Suite is an all-in-one platform for managing your freelance business. It helps you track projects, manage clients, generate invoices, and monitor your business performance.

We support multiple currencies including USD, EUR, GBP, JPY, CAD, AUD, ZAR, NGN, KES, GHS, BRL, MXN, SGD, and AED. You can select your preferred currency when creating invoices.

To create an invoice:
  1. Navigate to the Invoices section
  2. Click "New Invoice"
  3. Select a client and project
  4. Add invoice items and details
  5. Set the due date and currency
  6. Click "Create Invoice"

The Projects section allows you to:
  • Create and manage project milestones
  • Track time spent on tasks
  • Monitor project status and deadlines
  • View project-specific analytics
